Tour Examples

Spectacular Views Across Kyushu

Motonosumi Shrine - Yanagawa - Itoshima - Takachiho Gorge

A day full of cultural and natural beauty. From the striking red gates of Motonosumi Shrine to peaceful Yanagawa boat rides, seaside cafes in Itoshima, and the mystical cliffs of Takachiho Gorge.
→ A healing and inspiring journey that refreshes both heart and body.

Magnificent Nature and Hidden Power Spots

Mt. Aso - Tsunoshima Bridge - Kamishikimi Kumanoimasu Shrine

A journey through Kyushu’s dramatic landscapes. The expansive Aso grasslands and smoking volcanoes are awe-inspiring. Enjoy the cobalt blue sea views from Tsunoshima Bridge, and feel serenity at the sacred Kamishikimi shrine.
→ A route filled with breathtaking views and extraordinary atmosphere.
